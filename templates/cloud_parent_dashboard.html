Understanding Your Child's Progress

We are using a Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) assessment scale to track your child's growth. This scale measures how well your child has mastered specific skills throughout the term.

BE1 (Below Expectations - Beginning): Your child is taking the first steps in learning a new concept. At this stage, we are focusing on building a foundation and ensuring they understand the basic ideas.

BE2 (Below Expectations - Emerging): Your child is showing consistent effort and is beginning to grasp the fundamentals. With additional guided practice, they will continue to strengthen their understanding of these core skills.

ME1 (Meeting Expectations - Developing): Your child is making good progress and is successfully grasping the core concepts taught in class. They are on the right track and continuing to gain confidence.

ME2 (Meeting Expectations - Approaching): Your child is performing very well and is nearly at the point of full mastery. They are starting to handle the finer details of the work with ease.

EE1 (Exceeding Expectations - Proficient): Your child has successfully demonstrated a solid grasp of the competency. They are consistently showing that they have mastered the required skills.

EE2 (Exceeding Expectations - Advanced): Your child is performing at an outstanding level. They have moved beyond the basics and are showing a deep, creative understanding of the material.
