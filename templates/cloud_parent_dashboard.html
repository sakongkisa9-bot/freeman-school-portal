Summary for Parents: Understanding Your Child's Progress

We use a Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) assessment scale to track your child's growth. This scale measures how well your child has mastered specific skills throughout the term.

EE1 & EE2 (Exceeding Expectations): Your child has demonstrated a deep and outstanding understanding of the material. They are performing at an advanced level and are consistently showing mastery of the required skills.

ME1 & ME2 (Meeting Expectations): Your child is making good progress and is successfully grasping the core concepts taught in class. They are on the right track and continuing to gain confidence.

AE1 & AE2 (Approaching Expectations): Your child is working toward meeting the expected outcomes. They are beginning to handle the finer details of the work and are very close to mastering these competencies.

BE1 & BE2 (Below Expectations): Your child is in the early stages of learning these concepts. At this level, we are focusing on building a solid foundation and providing the necessary support to strengthen their understanding of the basics.
